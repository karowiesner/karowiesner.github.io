Karo's Fest

Birthday Celebration

June 26 - June 28, 2026

In the garden of
Karl-Marx-Str.32
Potsdam - Babelsberg

The weather will be hot. The garden is in the shade from about 3pm, the lake next to it and a little breeze should make it comfortable nevertheless.
'Difficult access' (see map) means 55 steps. Be careful when you walk down / up. Or take the entry via Wasserstrasse.
